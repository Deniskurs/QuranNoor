Features

Everything you need for your
spiritual journey

Built from the ground up as an Islamic companion — not an afterthought. Every feature designed with purpose.

Complete Quran

All 114 surahs with Uthmanic script, multiple translations, and verse-by-verse audio from renowned reciters.

Prayer Times

Precise calculations for your location. Multiple methods. Customizable notifications with authentic Adhan.

Qibla Compass

Real-time direction to the Kaaba with smooth animations. Distance to Makkah. Works offline.

Adhkar & Duas

Morning and evening adhkar. Fortress of the Muslim. 99 Names of Allah. Digital Tasbih counter.

Islamic Calendar

Hijri dates, Ramadan tracker, Suhoor & Iftar times, and important Islamic occasions.

Beautiful Themes

Light, Dark, Night, and Sepia themes. Dynamic Type support. Built for extended reading comfort.

Privacy

Your privacy is sacred

Qur'an Noor is built on a simple principle: your data belongs to you. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Prayer calculations happen entirely on-device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs.
